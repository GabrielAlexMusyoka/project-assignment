Introduction

Edward Okinda is a Kenyan motivational and inspirational speaker.
His main goal is to empower the youth to be positive and possess a future considerate mindset.
He has written several inspirational books eg,Inspiration to success first published in November 2004

Inspiration to success

Introduction

Edward Okinda in this book speaks about success and says that "success does not just happen. It is caused" and tries to prove by using different illustrations.
